How to use

PSN (Web):

  1. Visit the PlayStation website.
  2. Log in to your PSN account.
  3. From the top, click on the account profile menu.
  4. Choose redeem code.
  5. Enter your 12-digit code.
  6. Click on "Continue" and the balance will be added to your account.

PSN (Console):

  1. Open your console and log in to your PSN account.
  2. Go to PlayStation Store.
  3. From the top, click on the account profile menu.
  4. Choose redeem code.
  5. Enter your 12-digit code.
  6. Enter your digit code and press "Redeem".

PS App:

  1. Click on the store icon.
  2. From the top of the page, click on the menu and choose (Redeem Code).
  3. Enter your 12-digit code and press "Redeem."
